# MEDIC

## Monitoring for Elderly Daily Intervention & Care

MEDIC is a care support and monitoring app made for elderly patients, caregivers, and family members. It helps everyone stay connected through one shared system for daily care, medication tracking, wellness support, and appointment monitoring.

The app is designed to make everyday care easier to manage. Patients can keep up with their routines, caregivers can monitor and assist with daily needs, and family members can stay informed and involved.

## What MEDIC Introduces

- A role-based care experience for patients, caregivers, and family members
- A shared care-circle system that keeps the right people connected
- Medication tracking for daily adherence and monitoring
- Wellness routine support for healthier daily habits
- Appointment tracking for better care preparation and follow-up
- Dashboard summaries that make important care updates easier to see

## AI Functionality

MEDIC includes built-in AI features to support everyday care in a simple and helpful way.

- `Medic AI Chatbot` lets patients ask about their medications, routines, appointments, and health notes
- `Medic AI Coach` gives personalized wellness guidance based on the patient's current app data
- `AI Routine Generation` suggests gentle wellness routines that can be reviewed, edited, and saved into the app
- `Backup AI Guidance` keeps recommendations available from current patient data when live AI is unavailable
